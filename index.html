A blog about Web-Technology

This blog is powered by Bootstrap, SASS, Fontawesome and hard work!

My thoughts about frontend-frameworks and workflow in general.

This blog itself is just another manifestation of different approaches on how to create a website. Which frameworks and preprocessor should be used? Should I bother with commands in the terminal or just use GUI-applications for my workflow? What works best in a team? I want to give my humble opinion on that topic, since I´m quiet new to this field of work. My goal with this blog is to give you some insights to my workflow and the technologies I have already worked with. Maybe you can help me to improve here and there and if you find something useful on this site for yourself it´s even better. Have a great time!

How did I start developing websites and software?

Author: Dennis Loska, 11.12.2016

If you are new tro programming or web-development in general, you may ask yourself where to start. The best tip I can giver you is to just stick to the basics for a while, until you have mastered them all. If you wantr to become a frontend-developer for example, then master html, css and javascript first. You can worry about your workflow and which framework to use after that. If you want to challenge yourself - that´s great, but if the result is you quitting because of frustration then you should think about starting at a lower level, and raise the difficulty over time.
